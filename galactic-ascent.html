Mission Rules

Launch from the pad at 1 and race the grid to home base at 100. On your turn, roll and fly forward that many squares.

Board your ship and warp ahead to its lane's end. Good aliens beam you forward 3; bad aliens shove you back 3; a malfunction sends you sliding down a snake (small, mid or major).

Touch down on a planet to claim its badge. A square only triggers when you land on it by rolling — warps and shoves don't chain, but if a warp drops you onto a planet, you still grab that badge.

The mission ends the moment a crew member crosses 100. The winner is whoever holds the most points at that point (each world is worth points) — reaching home first only breaks a tie.
